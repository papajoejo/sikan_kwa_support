🔒 SikaNkwa Privacy Policy

Your privacy and data security are our top priorities

📱 App Overview

SikaNkwa is a habit and expense tracking app designed to help you build better financial habits and manage your money effectively. We believe in complete transparency about how we handle your data.

🔐 Data Collection & Storage

Local Storage Only: SikaNkwa stores all your data locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or store any of your personal information on external servers.

No Cloud Services: Your expense records, habit data, and personal preferences remain completely private and under your control.

No Analytics: We do not track your usage patterns or collect analytics data.

🌐 Internet Access

SikaNkwa requires internet access only for:

  • App updates from the App Store
  • Downloading the app initially

No personal data is transmitted during these processes.

📊 Permissions

SikaNkwa may request the following permissions:

  • Notifications: To send daily reminders for habit tracking (optional)
  • Local Storage: To save your app data on your device

You can disable any of these permissions at any time in your device settings.

🔄 Data Sharing

No Third-Party Sharing: We do not share your data with any third parties, advertisers, or external services.

No Social Media Integration: Your financial and habit data is never posted to social media platforms.

🛡️ Data Security

Device Security: Your data is protected by your device's built-in security features.

No External Access: Since data is stored locally, it cannot be accessed by external parties.

Backup Control: You control whether your data is included in device backups.

👥 Children's Privacy

SikaNkwa is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

📅 Policy Updates

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the app and on this page.
