‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add a new expense?

On the Dashboard, tap the "Add" button. Fill in the amount, select a category, add a note if desired, and tap "Save".

How do I create a new habit?

Go to the Habits tab and tap the "+" button. Enter a habit name and tap "Create". Mark it complete daily to build streaks.

Can I edit or delete expenses?

Yes! Tap the edit icon next to any expense to modify it, or swipe left on an expense to delete it.

How do I change the app theme?

Go to Settings and toggle between Light and Dark themes. Your preference will be saved automatically.

Can I change the currency?

Yes! In Settings, tap on Currency and select your preferred currency from the dropdown menu.

Where is my data stored?

All your data is stored locally on your device. We don't collect or store any information on external servers.

How do I sort my expenses?

In the "All Expenses" view, tap the sort icon in the top right to choose from different sorting options like date, category, or amount.

üîß Troubleshooting

App won't open

Try restarting your device. If the problem persists, delete and reinstall the app from the App Store.

Data disappeared

Check if you accidentally cleared app data in your device settings. Always backup your device regularly.

Notifications not working

Go to your device Settings > SikaNkwa > Notifications and ensure they're enabled.
